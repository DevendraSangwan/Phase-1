Description

Our resturent is a classic Italian pasta dish made with garlic, olive oil, and chili flakes. It's simple, quick, and full of flavor.

Pizza

  • 200 Aata
  • 4-6 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped parsley (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a pot, add salt, and cook spaghetti until al dente.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic slices, and cook until golden.
  3. Add red pepper flakes and cook for a few seconds.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the pan. Mix well with the oil and garlic.
  5. Add chopped parsley and toss well.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

Tip: Be careful not to burn the garlic, as it can make the dish bitter.
